About Us

SubscribeMe is a passionate team of developers, designers, and entrepreneurs committed to transforming how small and medium-sized service businesses manage their customers.
We believe in simple, smart, and scalable digital tools that empower businesses to focus on growth — not paperwork.

Our platform helps fitness clubs, learning centers, and coffee shops manage subscriptions, track customer visits via QR codes, and offer personalized services like schedules, diet plans, and loyalty discounts.
We are on a mission to make digital CRM accessible and affordable for all local businesses.
Driven by innovation, backed by data, and inspired by real-world needs — we build tools people actually use.
