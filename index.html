The Problem

Challenges facing small & medium businesses

Critical Impact

Lack of automation in counting customer visits

Businesses lose up to 15% of revenue due to manual tracking errors and forgotten check-ins.

Efficiency Gap

No ability to convert visitors into regular customers

Without loyalty data, 70% of first-time visitors never return back to the service.

Legacy System

Use of outdated methods like Excel or no control at all

Manual data entry wastes 10+ hours per week for business owners and staff.
